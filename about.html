The Genesis

Founded in 2019, SYNTHDATA emerged from a research initiative at the intersection of computational physics and civil engineering. The realization was stark: traditional crowd control was reactive, relying on physical barriers and human intervention after a crisis had already begun.

We pivoted to the "Synthetic First" approach—modeling the disaster to prevent it. By 2022, our simulations had been deployed in 14 major metropolitan hubs, reducing congestion-related incidents by an average of 64%.

Today, SYNTHDATA serves as the invisible backbone for global event planners, transit authorities, and government defense divisions. We do not provide software; we provide certainty.
