Protocol 01: Motion Entropy Detection

Quantum Displacement Mapping

Standard CCTV systems rely on pixel-change thresholds. If a pixel changes color, an alert is triggered. This leads to massive false-positive rates from environmental factors like wind, shadows, or rain.

Motion Entropy Detection (MED) analyzes the *path* and *energy* of movement. By calculating the entropy—the degree of disorder—within a moving cluster, our engine distinguishes between the mechanical, repetitive motion of a swaying tree and the calculated, purposeful displacement of a human actor.
